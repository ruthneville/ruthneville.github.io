About

I am a spatial data scientist specialising in population geography and demography, with a focus on housing and migration and mobility. My PhD research investigated international student mobility and the socio-economic, cultural, economic, and institutional factors shaping migration flows, with an emphasis on the impact of external shocks such as Brexit and the COVID-19 pandemic. My work employs advanced methodologies, including machine learning forecasting models and spatial interaction models to predict and measure population trends. I have also contributed to projects that leverage innovative spatial data sources, such as digital trace data, to analyse population movements during crises.
